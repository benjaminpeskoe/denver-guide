# ─── Restaurants ───

Snooze, an A.M. Eatery
restaurant, LoDo
kid-friendly, open-early
Best breakfast burrito in Denver. Go early on weekends — line gets long by 9am.

Guard and Grace
restaurant, LoDo
date-night
High-end steakhouse. The happy hour is an incredible deal.

Hop Alley
restaurant, RiNo
date-night
Chinese-American food in a cool RiNo space. Get the dan dan noodles.

Uncle Ramen
restaurant, RiNo
friend-rec
Haven't been yet but multiple people have told me to go.

# ─── Coffee ───

Good Bread
coffeeshop, RiNo
pet-friendly, kid-friendly, outdoor-seating, open-early
Great pastries and sandwiches — get there early, they're sold out of the best stuff by mid/late morning. On Friday nights they do pretty decent pizza and dinner. Nice patio that's great for dogs and strollers.

Huckleberry Roasters
coffeeshop, RiNo
outdoor-seating
My go-to coffee shop. Great space to work from too.

# ─── Outdoors ───

Red Rocks Trading Post Trail
hike, Morrison
kid-friendly, pet-friendly, free
Easy loop with incredible rock formations. Great for all ages.

Cheesman Park
park, Cheesman Park
pet-friendly, kid-friendly, free
Great views of the mountains. The pavilion area is beautiful at sunset.

# ─── Attractions ───

Denver Art Museum
attraction, Golden Triangle
kid-friendly
World-class collection. The Hamilton Building architecture alone is worth the visit.
