# ─── Restaurants ───

Bamboo Sushi
restaurant, Congress Park
kid-friendly
Great sushi, laid back vibe, surprisingly tolerant of kids.

Chef Zorba's
restaurant, Congress Park
kid-friendly
Laid back Greek diner with huge portions. They have crayons for kids.

Peter's Chinese
restaurant, Congress Park
My favorite Chinese takeout in Denver. They have a few tables but don't plan to eat here.

Spice Room
restaurant, Colfax
Excellent Indian. Great for takeout or sit down.

Rosenberg's Bagels at Stanley Marketplace
restaurant, Aurora
kid-friendly
Good bagels with fish and sandwich options. Stanley Marketplace is a great place to go with kids and hang out for a few hours.

Sap Sua
restaurant, Congress Park
Modern, very delicious Vietnamese food. They have a great bakery on Saturday mornings. Fine for adults but not really a kid spot.

Little Arthur's Hoagies
restaurant, Capitol Hill
Delicious, messy, enormous sandwiches. The chicken cutlet with pistachio pesto is worth a trip for. Tap room in the same space.

Curtis Park Deli
restaurant, Curtis Park
Nothing fancy but really solid sandwiches.

Molotov
restaurant, City Park
Homey feeling Ukrainian restaurant. Great vibes.

Restaurant Olivia
restaurant, Washington Park
date-night
Delicious pasta. Great for a date night.

Lucina
restaurant, Park Hill
Latin-American and Spanish food. Very good. Kind of loud.

Bonnie Brae Ice Cream
restaurant, Bonnie Brae
kid-friendly
Best ice cream in Denver (IMO).

Mister Oso (RiNo)
restaurant, RiNo
Good tacos and other Mexican/Latin-American food. Not really authentic but still good. Kind of loud but fun.

Mister Oso (Wash Park)
restaurant, Washington Park
Good tacos and other Mexican/Latin-American food. Not really authentic but still good. Kind of loud but fun.

Carmine Lonardo's
restaurant, Lakewood
Very authentic Italian deli. Great, huge sandwiches and lots of Italian ingredients you wouldn't find elsewhere. Surprising to find a place like this outside of New York.

# ─── Coffee ───

Goodbread
coffeeshop, Colfax
pet-friendly, kid-friendly, outdoor-seating, open-early
Great pastries and sandwiches — get there early, they're sold out of the best stuff by mid/late morning. On Friday nights they do pretty decent pizza and dinner. Nice patio that's great for dogs and strollers.

Honey Hill
coffeeshop|restaurant, Park Hill
Fancy, homey feeling coffee shop with breakfast sandwiches.

Novo Coffee
coffeeshop, Congress Park
outdoor-seating
Really nice patio and good drinks. No cold brew.

Lula Rose
coffeeshop, Colfax
Sweet little coffee shop on Colfax. Owned by the same people as Goodbread.

# ─── Outdoors ───

Cheesman Park
park, Cheesman Park
pet-friendly, kid-friendly, free
Great views of the mountains. The pavilion area is beautiful at sunset.

Highline Canal Trail
hike|park, Cherry Hills Village
kid-friendly, pet-friendly, free
Flat trail for walking, running, and biking. Best entry near Kent School or Marjorie Perry Nature Preserve.

Mount Falcon
hike, Morrison
kid-friendly, free
One of the closest hiking areas to Denver but never feels crowded. There's a very accessible ~3 mile loop you can do pretty much all year round.

Chavez & Beaver Brook Trail
hike, Genesee
free
Beautiful 5 mile loop through a pretty canyon. Can get crowded and very hot on summer days — start early.

Chautauqua Park
hike|park, Boulder
pet-friendly, free
Really nice hiking area near Boulder. Lots of different trails to explore.

# ─── Attractions ───

Denver Zoo
attraction, City Park
kid-friendly, open-early
We spend a ton of time here. Opens early for members. The reptile and amphibian house is great for cold days.

Denver Museum of Nature & Science
attraction, City Park
kid-friendly
Can be crowded but very entertaining for kids.

Denver Central Library
attraction, Downtown
kid-friendly, free
Cool space to hang out. Great children's section with toys. Interesting historical books and maps area.

Morrison Nature Center
attraction, Aurora
kid-friendly, free
Great for a nice day. Children's play area, some trails, and some indoor displays.

Dazzle Jazz Club
attraction, Downtown
date-night
Decent acts come through here. The vibe is only OK but the music sounds good.

Red Rocks Amphitheater
attraction, Morrison
Legendary music venue for a reason. Getting here is a total pain.

Ball Arena
attraction, LoDo
Perfectly acceptable place to watch a basketball game. Jokic is fun to see in person.
